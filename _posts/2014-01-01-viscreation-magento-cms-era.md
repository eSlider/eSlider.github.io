## Summary

**~1,900+ author commits** across VisCreation-era work: in-house **VisBack** (2006) and **VisMin / VisCMS** (2007) PHP CMS; Magento e-commerce (~1,100 commits on opticfriends + testorino); early **produktor.io** geospatial stack.

Current platform work continues under [produktor.io platform](/posts/produktor-platform-self-hosted-stack/).
